Your Journey | Your Map | Your Memories

My Travel Map is more than just a travel planner — it's a simple and visual space to organize your travel ideas, discover new destinations, and turn your dreams into meaningful journeys and memories.

Our mission is to inspire and empower travelers to explore the world at their own pace and visualize their journeys in a meaningful way.

Who is it for?

The platform is designed for users like Shiri Cohen — an organized and curious traveler who loves planning trips and keeping everything in one clean place.

For Shiri, a journey doesn't start at the airport, it begins with the very first location she discovers. She believes the magic of travel lies in the details - from a perfectly curated itinerary to the aesthetic of every saved moment.

Who is Shiri?

Shiri represents our main user persona.

She is a busy administrative manager who enjoys traveling in her free time. She is creative, detail-oriented, and loves planning her next destination in advance.

At the same time, she prefers simple, clean and non-complicated digital tools.

For Shiri, travel is not only about the trip itself — it starts with inspiration, continues with planning, and becomes a collection of memories afterward.
